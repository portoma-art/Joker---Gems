Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

This Privacy Document governs the operational architecture of Joker Gems, an interactive logic software utility constructed by Serhii Polovko.

1. Definitive Data Commitment

The core operating philosophy of Joker Gems relies on absolute user privacy. The application is engineered to perform entirely on-device. We do not gather, compile, store, monitor, or process any categories of your personal identity information, analytical metadata, or in-app usage sessions.

2. Third-Party Restrictions

Because zero information is gathered by the architecture, no metrics or parameters are ever shared, distributed, or commercialized with external entities, analytics institutions, or third-party advertising companies.

3. Local Sandbox Storage

Any settings or progress state modifications generated during gameplay loop interactions are managed directly inside the internal ecosystem of your hardware platform. This information remains completely private and unreachable to us.

4. Policy Updates

We maintain the authority to revise this regulatory overview as platform rules alter. We suggest periodic inspection of this text to maintain awareness regarding our data management standard.

5. Communication Portal

For administrative queries concerning this non-collection mandate, submit inquiries to our support node: portoma@atomicmail.io.
